## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How do I log in or reset my password?">
Accounts are created using your school email. If you forget your password, follow the reset steps [here](/media/getting-started/resetting-password).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use Stack without the Equipment Checkout system?">
Yes. Media Essentials and Equipment Checkout are separate modules and can be used independently or together.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does the leaderboard work?">
Ranking is based on activity, consistency, and submission quality. See [how to rank up](/media/profiles/how-to-rank-up).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I view someone else's submissions or profile?">
Go to the [Leaderboard](/media/profiles/leaderboard-overview) or use [Advanced Search](/media/search/advanced-search).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if gear is lost or damaged?">
Use the [Mark Missing](/checkout/process/mark-missing) feature or speak with your advisor to report issues.
</Accordion>
